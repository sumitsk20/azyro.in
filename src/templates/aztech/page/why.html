3Pillars of Sustainability

The 3 P’s, People, Planet and Profit are critical aspects for sustainability of development. The Social aspect, ‘People’, measure quality of life; the Environmental aspect, ‘Planet’, captures factors related to energy, pollution and emissions; the Economic aspect, ‘Profit’, assess the economic health.


Circular Economy - Zero Waste Solutions

Undoubtedly, the growth of linear economy produces faster results leading to adoption by developing countries. Linear economy is not sustainable though and can lead to disastrous results, often ending up in failure and a literal ‘mess’.

At Azyro, we believe that for the stable growth of India, a circular economy is to be adopted and waste management is a critical part of it.
