Internship

The internship programme at Azyro Infratech, has been designed to hone up the skills of young persons and give shape to their ideas about the waste management sector providing them on-ground experience, before they launch themselves on a well-defined career choice. It is a one year long open ended programme commencing on the 01st of every month through the year.
