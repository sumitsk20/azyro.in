ABOUT US

Azyro Infratech is one of the leading consulting firms in the field of Solid Waste Management, Sustainability, Renewable Energy, Engineering & Design, Sanitation and Waste Water Sector. We specialize in providing a complete Integrated Waste Management services with the strategy of reducing and recycling waste and target to fulfill our client’s expectations. We expertise in developing and producing engineering designs, drawings & schematic schedules. We develop innovative, efficient solutions that create value for our clients, by using leading-edge technology and the right resources.

Reduce

Wherever possible, waste reduction is the preferable option.

REUSE

If waste is produced, every effort should be made to reuse it if practicable.

RECYCLE

Recycling should only be considered for waste which cannot be reduced or reused.

RECOVER

Finally, it may be possible to recover materials or energy from waste which cannot be reduced, reused or recycled.
