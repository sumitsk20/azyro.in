We constantly adapt from the changing scenario of the world, ambitions of our governments and the need of our growing society and communities for a better quality of life.

We believe in the strategy of 4R system for effective Waste Management System.

Reduce

Wherever possible, waste reduction is the preferable option.

REUSE

If waste is produced, every effort should be made to reuse it if practicable.

RECYCLE

Recycling should only be considered for waste which cannot be reduced or reused.

RECOVER

Finally, it may be possible to recover materials or energy from waste which cannot be reduced, reused or recycled.
